Bigger & Better

Last year, we hosted Chicago Hacks, Chicago’s largest high school hackathon. With over 130 attendees and over 30 projects presented, it would be an understatement to call that just a success. We saw passionate hackers collaborate and build amazing things in 24 hours. Through workshops and mentorships, beginners and veteran programmers created awesome projects.

What is Hack Club?

Collaborate & Innovate

You will have 24 hours to build an awesome project with your friends. You've been learning skills in school that you haven't put into use - we help solve that problem. We’d love to see you convert ideas into apps and games, and it’s also a great time to learn new skills through our variety of workshops.
